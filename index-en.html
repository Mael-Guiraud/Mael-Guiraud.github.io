Profile

As a researcher and lecturer, I combine teaching, research, and scientific collaboration, exploring current challenges in emerging technologies.

Research Areas

  • Network Optimization:
    • Work on deterministic periodic flow scheduling in collaboration with Nokia Bell Labs France.
    • Development of academic and industrial projects to enhance communication network performance.
  • Smart Buildings:
    • Research projects on using physical and comfort data for sustainable building management.
    • Exploration of IoT and AI technologies to optimize user comfort and reduce energy consumption.
  • Predictive Maintenance:
    • Supervision of a Ph.D. project on reinforcement learning to predict failures in smart buildings.
    • Proposing innovative solutions despite the lack of historical data.
